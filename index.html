Top three palces to visit in Gdansk

Go for a stroll in the historic Old Town.

A must-see for anyone visiting the city. Explore the colourful facades of the Long Market, the stunning St. Mary's Church, and the towering Town Hall.

Stocznia Gdanska - the birth place of modern Poland.

Wander around the iconic site where 'Solidarity' was born.

Grab a coffee on Mariacka Street.

One of the most stunning streets in Gdańsk, this cobbled street is lined with amber galleries and cafés and runs from St. Mary's Basilica to the Motława River.

Top three cocktail bars

Flisak '76

A cozy basement with a beautiful soul, remaining in the hands of one family since 1976. The menus and signature cocktails are always themed. Currently, it is a fairy tale journey to the land of "Once upon a time...".

Mala Sztuka

A charming cocktail bar located in the heart of Gdansk's Old Town, with an atmospheric outdoor area just next to the river.

TLEN

Try their menu "Regional Atlas" inspired by regions in Poland, where each of the cocktails represents a unique flavour and characteristic ingredients from a given area.
